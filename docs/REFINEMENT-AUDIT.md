# Refinement Audit — Production-Ready SaaS Site

## PART 1 — GAP ANALYSIS

### Weak Copy Sections

| Section | Issue | Recommendation |
|---------|-------|----------------|
| **Hero** | "Never Miss Another New Patient Call" is strong but subheadline is generic | Add dentist-specific outcome: "Capture every inquiry. Book more appointments. 24/7." |
| **Trust Bar** | "Partner" placeholders lack credibility | Replace with "Trusted by dental clinics" + real or aspirational metrics |
| **Problem** | Stats (120/35/20) feel made up without source | Add "Based on typical practice data" or cite survey |
| **ROI** | "10x–30x" is broad; needs clearer math | Show: $3,750 extra ÷ $99 = 38x. Be specific |
| **Pricing** | Starter vs Growth differentiation unclear | Add "Best for" line: "Starter: single location" / "Growth: multi-location" |

### Missing Trust Signals

- **Named testimonials:** Current quotes lack names, roles, practices
- **Case study metrics:** "35% more booked" needs before/after numbers
- **Integration logos:** Text list only—need Google Calendar, etc. logos
- **Implementation timeline:** "1–3 days" in FAQ but not prominent
- **Security badges:** SOC 2, HIPAA mention if applicable

### Missing UI Visualization

- **Product Demo:** Video placeholder only—no actual embed
- **Chat interface:** No screenshot of AI conversation
- **Booking flow:** No calendar/booking UI mockup
- **Dashboard:** No analytics screenshot
- **Call flow:** No phone/call handling UI

### Unclear Product Explanation

- **"AI Employee" vs "AI Receptionist":** Terms used interchangeably—clarify: "AI Employee is our platform; AI Receptionist is the dental module"
- **Channels:** List exists but no visual of how they connect
- **Handoff:** "Escalate to staff"—how? When? Unclear

### SEO Gaps

- **Homepage title:** Should include "for dental clinics" for primary keyword
- **Dentist page:** Needs more H2s for long-tail (e.g., "AI receptionist for dental practices")
- **/ai-receptionist-vs-human:** Page exists as vs-front-desk; need vs-human variant
- **Internal linking:** Dentist page should link to /demo, /ai-receptionist, /best-ai-receptionist

### AI Search (GEO) Gaps

- **Definition blocks:** "What is an AI Receptionist?" exists but could be more quotable
- **Comparison content:** "AI Receptionist vs Hiring Staff" section exists; ensure it's crawlable
- **Structured answers:** FAQ answers should be 2–3 sentences for citation

### LinkedIn Funnel Gaps

- **No dedicated /demo page:** Demo video lives in homepage anchor; cold traffic needs standalone page
- **Dentist page doesn't lead to demo:** Should have "Watch 2-min demo" → /demo
- **Form placement:** Demo form appears late; consider sticky CTA on mobile
- **"What to expect"** on demo: Not prominent enough

---

## Specific Recommendations

1. **Create /demo page** — Hero, 2-min video, how it works, example conversation, calendar widget
2. **Add product screenshots** — AI chat, call assistant, booking flow, lead qualifier, analytics
3. **Strengthen GEO sections** — Ensure "What is an AI Receptionist?", "How AI Receptionists Work", "AI Receptionist vs Hiring Staff" are prominent and well-structured
4. **Enhance dentist page** — Make it the primary LinkedIn landing page with clear funnel to /demo
5. **Add TrustSignals to homepage** — Testimonials, case studies, integrations, security
6. **Add /ai-receptionist-vs-human** — Or enhance ai-receptionist-vs-front-desk with "vs human" content
7. **Update schema** — Ensure Organization, SoftwareApplication, Product, FAQPage all present
8. **Sticky CTA** — "Book Demo" bar on scroll for mobile
